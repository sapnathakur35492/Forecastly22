Your Report, in 5 Simple Steps

Each step is designed to give you full control over what you get, at a price you choose — with zero lock-ins or hidden costs.

1

Search any market

Type any market, industry, or niche into the search bar on our homepage. Our engine instantly recognizes the sector and prepares a structured report scope — no need to browse a catalog or fill out an inquiry form.

You can search for anything: from broad sectors like "Renewable Energy" to ultra-specific niches like "Solid-State Battery for Electric Vehicles" or "AI-Powered Drug Discovery Platforms."

Any market or niche No catalog browsing Instant scope generation
Try searches like: "Electric Vehicle Battery Market", "Cybersecurity SaaS", "Digital Therapeutics", "Semiconductor Wafer"
2

Configure your report

Once inside the report builder, you get full control over every parameter. This is where you decide exactly what data you need — nothing more, nothing less.

Geography: Select the Global overview ($20), tick individual countries ($20 each), or select entire regions. We cover 17 countries across 5 regions — North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, Middle East & Africa, and Latin America. Need a country not in the list? Add it as a custom entry.

Segmentation: Toggle on 3-level market segmentation (+$99 flat fee). Our engine auto-generates segment categories based on your market — for example, an EV report gets "By Vehicle Type", "By Component", and "By End-User". Segmentation data is applied at global, regional, and country level.

17 default countries Custom country support 3-level segmentation Multi-currency Revenue + Volume Up to 10Y forecast

Before spending a single rupee, download a free demo Excel to see exactly what your report will look like. The demo contains the complete structure — every sheet, every table, every column — but with placeholder "xx.xx" values instead of actual data.

This lets you verify that the report covers what you need: the right countries, the right segments, the right time horizon. If anything needs adjustment, change your configuration and download another demo — as many times as you want.

Stripe & PayPal Auto-cap at $199 / $299 No hidden fees Instant confirmation

Analyst review & delivery in 60 minutes

This is what sets us apart. After payment, your report doesn't get auto-generated and dumped into your inbox. Instead, our in-house research analysts take over:

What's Inside Your Excel Report

Every report is a multi-sheet .xls workbook with structured data tables, styled formatting, and source attribution. Here's exactly what each section contains.

🏠

Home Sheet — Report Summary

The first sheet gives you a complete overview of your report configuration at a glance.

  • Report title and market description
  • Selected countries and regions
  • Forecast period and base year
  • Metric (Revenue/Volume/Both) and currency
  • Segmentation status and pricing summary
  • Branded estimately header with report metadata
🌐

Global Sheet — Worldwide Market

The global overview of your market with total market size, regional breakdown, and segment analysis.

  • Global revenue line (base year through forecast)
  • Region-by-region breakdown table (if countries selected)
  • CAGR column for each row
  • Segment tables (if segmentation is ON) — one per level
  • Source attribution row below each table
🌍

Regional Sheets — Country Breakdowns

One dedicated sheet for each region you selected, containing country-level granularity.

  • Regional total revenue/volume line
  • Country-by-country table within that region
  • Per-country segmentation tables (Enterprise)
  • Custom countries appear alongside default countries
  • Consistent formatting across all regional sheets
🧩

Segmentation — 3 Levels of Depth

When segmentation is enabled, you get three layers of market breakdown — tailored to your specific market.

  • Auto-generated based on your report title keywords
  • Example: EV market → By Vehicle Type, By Component, By End-User
  • Applied at global, regional, AND country level
  • Each segment table includes items, yearly data, and CAGR
  • Analyst-verified for relevance before delivery
💰

Revenue Data — Market Size in Currency

When Revenue is selected as your metric, every table shows market value in your chosen currency.

  • Available in USD, EUR, GBP, or JPY
  • Values displayed in Millions (Billions for JPY)
  • Year-by-year estimates from base year to forecast end
  • CAGR calculated from base to final year
  • Total row with aggregated figures
📦

Volume Data — Units & Tonnage

When Volume is selected, tables show physical quantities instead of currency — ideal for manufacturing and commodity markets.

  • Measured in Units or Tons (market-dependent)
  • Same structure as revenue: yearly + CAGR
  • When "Both" is selected: revenue table first, then volume table directly below
  • Both metrics in one workbook, no separate files
